Stewardship at scale is built from small repetitions multiplied by many people.

## Start here

1. Plant native, not pretty.
2. Leave leaf litter — it's a nursery, not mess.
3. Buy less, mend more.
4. Vote for protected land.
5. Walk into a forest and learn the name of three things.

None of these are heroic. All of them, done by enough people, are.
