## The web is denser than it looks

A single old-growth tree can host more than **1,000 distinct species** — birds, beetles, lichens, fungi — wired together by chemistry and chance. Remove one strand and the whole web answers.

## By the numbers

- Known species on Earth: **~2.1 million**
- Estimated total (most still undescribed): **8.7 million**
- Distinct forest biomes: **12**

## Why it matters

When a single keystone species disappears — a wolf, a sea otter, a fig wasp — the loss cascades through the network for decades. Restoration is easier the earlier you notice.
