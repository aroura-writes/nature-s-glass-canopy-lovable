Oceans take another 25% of human carbon output, paying for it with acidifying water. Climate stability isn't an abstraction; it is the daily breathing of ecosystems we are still learning to measure.

## Numbers to hold

- Annual forest CO₂ uptake: **7.6 Gt**
- Human CO₂ absorbed by oceans: **25%**
- Tree species worldwide: **73,000+**
